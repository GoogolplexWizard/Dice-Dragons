How I will approach the problem

I can execute this problem by having a few days a month were kids can come and play Dungeons & Dragons (D&D). This event would be called Dice & Dragons. Kids can come and play D&D twice a month at the library. D&D is a way for kids to interact with other kids by playing a character in a story that the dungeon master tells. The story will have a theme that we will decide it might be in a fairy tale or a video game. The goal is for kids to start playing D&D and interacting with other people.
