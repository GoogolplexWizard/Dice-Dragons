My Goal

At the conclusion of this, I hope that kids will not play video game all the time but also play with their friends and family. I hope they will play D&D at home with their family. They could also play at school with their friends. I also hope that they will get D&D books and play all the time. I also hope that they enjoy it and continue playing it as a dungeon master or as a player. In conclusion of all of this I hope that kids will get off their devices and play Dungeons & Dragons with their friends and family.
