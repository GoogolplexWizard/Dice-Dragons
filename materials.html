Materials

In order to implement this plan, I will need. A place to meet a library. Books and supplies: the three core rulebooks, any other rulebooks or D&D books need to play, a story pre-made or homebrew, and character sheets for the players to use. I will also need flyers and posters to get the word out. I will need 4 kids at the least. I will also need other dungeon masters just depending on how many kids want to play. All of this is important, but the most important is that they have fun playing D&D.
